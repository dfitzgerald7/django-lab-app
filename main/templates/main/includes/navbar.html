How to use this app.
Each user has many labs that have many todo items.
Once you are logged in, you can create a lab.
In the new lab form, you can add as many todo items as needed.
Completed labs are displayed as green while incomplete labs are red.
When a todo item for a lab is completed, click on the lab and check the task.
Once a lab has all of its todo items completed, it will be displayed in green.
